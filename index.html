Internet Of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a system of interrelated connected devices that can collect and exchange data.

  1. Smart Home Devices: These include smart thermostats, lighting systems, and security cameras that can be controlled remotely.
  2. Wearable Technology: Devices like smartwatches and fitness trackers that monitor health and fitness data.
  3. Industrial IoT: Sensors and devices used in manufacturing and industrial processes to improve efficiency and safety.
  4. Connected Vehicles: Cars and other vehicles equipped with internet connectivity for navigation, entertainment, and safety features.

The Internet of Things (IoT) has the potential to revolutionize various industries by enabling seamless communication and data exchange between devices.
